## SHOW ME / TELL ME PAGE

Vehicle safety check questions for Category B vehicles (effective July 1, 2008). 13 question combinations that candidates may be asked during the practical driving test.

### Combination 1
- **B08 (Show):** Show me how you would check that the direction indicators are working. (Activate indicators and verify all function)
- **B10 (Tell):** Tell me how you would check that the brakes are working before starting a journey. (Brakes should feel firm, function properly during travel, not cause vehicle pull)

### Combination 2
- **B05 (Show):** Locate windscreen washer reservoir and explain level verification methods
- **B09 (Show):** Demonstrate brake light functionality using reflections or assistance from others

### Combination 3
- **B02 (Show/Tell):** Check power steering by applying wheel pressure during engine start or turning wheel immediately after moving
- **B12 (Tell):** Find recommended tyre pressures in manufacturer's documentation; check pressure with gauge when tyres are cold

### Combination 4
- **B04 (Show):** Apply parking brake firmly to demonstrate it secures vehicle without excessive travel
- **B05 (Show):** Windscreen washer reservoir identification and level checking

### Combination 5
- **B01 (Show):** Open bonnet, locate dipstick/oil indicator, describe checking against minimum/maximum markers
- **B12 (Tell):** Tyre pressure information and checking procedures

### Combination 6
- **B03 (Show):** Locate engine coolant reservoir markings and describe topping-up procedure
- **B18 (Tell):** Head restraint should align with eye level or ear height, positioned close to head back

### Combination 7
- **B07 (Show):** Identify brake fluid reservoir; verify adequate level against marked indicators
- **B13 (Tell):** Tyres require 1.6mm tread depth centrally and around circumference; no cuts or bulges

### Combination 8
- **B06 (Show):** Demonstrate horn functionality using appropriate controls (off-road only)
- **B13 (Tell):** Tyre safety standards (tread depth and condition requirements)

### Combination 9
- **B10 (Tell):** Brake check requirements before journey
- **B14 (Show):** Operate windscreen washer and wiper controls to clean windscreen

### Combination 10
- **B11 (Tell):** Operate switch (turn on ignition if necessary), then walk round vehicle
- **B15 (Show):** Set demister controls for front and rear window clearing using fan, temperature, air direction

### Combination 11
- **B16 (Show):** Activate rear fog light; explain appropriate usage conditions
- **B19 (Tell):** Anti-lock braking system faults indicated by warning light illumination

### Combination 12
- **B09 (Show):** Brake light verification using reflections or assistant help
- **B11 (Tell):** Headlight and tail light checking procedures

### Combination 13
- **B17 (Show):** Switch headlights from dipped to main beam; main beam warning light confirms activation
- **B19 (Tell):** Anti-lock braking system problem identification via warning light
