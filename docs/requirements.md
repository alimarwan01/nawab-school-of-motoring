# Requirements

## What This Is
A modern, professional website for **Nawab School of Motoring** — a driving school based in Leicester (also serving Luton). This is a redesign of the existing site at nawabmotoring.co.uk which looks dated and unprofessional.

## Who Uses It
- **Learner drivers** in Leicester/Luton looking for driving lessons
- **The instructor (Nawab)** who needs a site that builds trust and generates calls/bookings

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- Hero section with tagline: "Friendly, Punctual, Patient & Professional Instruction"
- Starting price callout: lessons from GBP28/hr
- Prominent call-to-action: phone number 07977 589 345
- Key selling points: DVSA approved, high first-time pass rate, modern dual-controlled vehicles, flexible scheduling (evenings/weekends at no extra cost), door-to-door service
- Featured testimonials (3-4 rotating)
- Quick links to services and pricing

### 2. Services Page
- Service types: Beginner lessons, Intensive courses, Refresher training, Theory test prep, Motorway instruction, Pass Plus
- Key features: Block booking discounts, Door-to-door service, Air-conditioned car, No extra cost for evenings/weekends
- Male and female instructors available

### 3. Pricing Page
- Hourly rates table (GBP30 Leicester, GBP35 outside, GBP45 motorway, GBP37 refresher)
- Block booking discounts (5hr/10hr/20hr packages)
- Student rates (GBP280 for 10 hours)
- Pass Plus course (GBP225 for 6 hours)
- Test day car hire rates (Cannock, Wigston, Outside Leicester — weekday/weekend/early morning)
- Terms & conditions note

### 4. Testimonials Page
- Grid/cards of student reviews (38+ reviews)
- Student name, quote, result (e.g. "Passed first time with 0 faults")
- Load more functionality

### 5. Show Me / Tell Me Page
- 13 vehicle safety check question combinations for the practical test
- Clean, easy-to-read format with expandable sections
- Helpful for students preparing for their test

### 6. Test Routes Page
- 16 Leicester driving test routes from Cannock Street test centre
- Route descriptions with key roads and manoeuvres
- Helpful reference for test preparation

### 7. Contact Page
- Phone number prominently displayed: 07977 589 345
- Address: 201 Sandhills Avenue, Hamilton, Leicester LE5 1QL
- Embedded Google Map
- Social links: Facebook (NawabMotoring), Twitter (@NawabMotoring)
- Simple contact form (name, phone, email, message)

## Design Direction
- Clean, modern, trustworthy — not flashy, not cheap-looking
- Professional colour scheme (dark navy/blue + white + accent colour)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Big readable text, generous whitespace
- Trust signals throughout (DVSA badge, pass rates, testimonials)
- Easy to find the phone number from any page
